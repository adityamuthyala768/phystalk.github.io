About Us

Phystalk is a student-led initiative dedicated to making advanced physics concepts accessible to curious learners everywhere. Founded by Adithya Muthyala and Samar Singh, Phystalk offers open, user-friendly research resources designed for all audiences. We curate specialized bibliographies on pioneering physicists, highlighting their discoveries and explaining their relevance both conceptually and mathematically. To make learning more engaging, we also create interactive games and share content across platforms such as YouTube and Instagram.

Our Goal

Our mission is to bridge the gap between high school understanding and research-level physics, presenting complex theories in a clear, approachable, and engaging way. We provide research that not only explains ideas but also demonstrates the calculations and reasoning behind every theory and law.

Have an idea of your own but not sure how to test it? Phystalk offers a platform for inquisitive minds to share, refine, and debate their thoughts. Through our Discord server, we encourage open discussions on theories, innovative ideas, and even controversies in physics.

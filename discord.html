Community Guidelines

1

Be Respectful

Treat all members with respect and courtesy. No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

2

Stay On Topic

Keep discussions relevant to the channel topic. Use #off-topic for non-physics conversations.

3

No Plagiarism

Don't copy homework or ask others to complete assignments for you. We help you learn, not cheat.

4

Quality Content

Share high-quality content and resources. Avoid spam, low-effort posts, or repetitive questions.

5

Use Search

Search previous messages before asking questions. Many common questions have been answered.

6

Constructive Help

When helping others, guide them to understand rather than just giving answers.

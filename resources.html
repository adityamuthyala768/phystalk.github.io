Contribution Guidelines

Organize Properly

Place files in the correct folders (Research Papers, Articles, Tutorials, Videos, Books, Tools). Create subfolders for specific topics when needed.

Use Descriptive Names

Name files clearly: "Quantum_Entanglement_Review_Nature_2023.pdf" rather than "paper1.pdf". Include author and publication year when possible.

Respect Copyright

Only upload content you have permission to share or that's freely available. Include proper attribution and source information.

Quality Over Quantity

Focus on high-quality, educational content that benefits the physics community. Avoid duplicates and low-quality materials.
